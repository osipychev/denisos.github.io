Development Progress

This simulation is being developed to testify dynamic mission planning in contested environments with autonomous systems. The capture-theflag game, where robotic teams compete to achieve their mission goal before the other. Game consists of eighteen autonomous systems, half being quad-rotors and the other half ground-based robots, split into three teams. Our approach to mission planning for these teams will include strategy supervision and low-level execution.
