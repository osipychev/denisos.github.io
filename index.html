About Me

Who Is That Guy?

Hi, I'm Denis Osipychev. I am an enthusiast in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Reinforcement Learning (RL), and Machine Learning (ML). I develop complex systems that can solve non-trivial tasks, adapt to changes in the enviroment, make sequential decisions, and even compete with a human. Does this sound exciting? Believe me, it truly does.

Currently, I am an AI Software Engineer at Boeing Research and Technology (BR&T) located in Huntsville, Alabama. Beside my main job, I am also a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My research is in multi-agent decision making, collaborative deep learning, and optimization.

I've received my M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Oklahoma State University where I worked on autonomous driving, behavior recognition, and human-in-the-loop systems.
