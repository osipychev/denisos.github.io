Denis Osipychev

Denis Osipychev is currently a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. He received his M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Oklahoma State University in 2015. His research interests are in Artificial Intelligence, Reinforcement Learning, Deep Learning and human-like intelligence.

Projects

I am currently involved in multiple academic and open-source projects.

Agrobot, Terra-Mepp

TERRA-MEPP is a low-cost, autonomous robot that analyzes biofuel crops throughout the growing season. The robot’s sensors collect an unprecedented amount of field data, and high-throughput analytical strategies quickly analyze it to pinpoint plants with desirable yield and sustainability traits.

Capture the Flag
